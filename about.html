Our Story

Qtec Solution Limited was founded at the end of 2019 with an aim to engineer robust technology services for super growing businesses founded by visionaries. We served software solutions for over 30 clients in the last two years and completed 50+ Medium and Large projects. Most of Qtec Solution Limited’s team members have very good experience of working with local and international clients in software development projects. On Time delivery and aligning with client’s requirements is something we are all about.

We got featured!

We have been featured at the daily famous newspaper in Bangladesh. They have acknowledged our good work and inspired us with the publication. On the Interview our Managing Director Mr. Mosharrof Hossain talked about How Qtec started their journey, what challenges Qtec faced over the years and how Qtec overcame those challenges. He also talked about how Qtec substantially grew its business during Covid-19 pandemic. On the right, you can see our team’s group photo.
