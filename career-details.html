Who We Are Looking For?

We are looking for a couple of UI Designers to produce high-quality user interface elements for our products.

Job Responsibilities

  • Illustrate design ideas using storyboards, process flows, and site mapping
  • Design graphic user interface elements, like layouts, banners, and marketing materials
  • Develop UI mockups and prototypes of different products
  • Conduct layout adjustments based on user feedback
  • Assist in the creation of a standardized library of branded UI components
  • Adhere to style standards on fonts, colors, and images

Skills & Qualifications

  • Proficient with visual design programs such as Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, etc
  • 1+ year of demonstrated experience in creating and implementing UI design
  • Ability to work effectively in a collaborative environment to create top-performing interfaces
  • Continued education and research into UI trends and current design strategy and technology

Educational Requirements

  • Graduation in any design discipline is preferred. However, we are looking for skills, not degrees.

Additional Job Requirements

  • Energetic, confident, and proactive
  • Ability to work independently and under pressure to undertake workload.
